INTRODUCTION to SMART MONEY CONCEPT

SMC is not just a Forex trading strategy, but an entire philosophy about how the markets work. Basically, SMC states that market makers.

(i.e., banks, hedge funds, etc.) are manipulative entities, and that moreover, they are actively making life difficult for retail traders.
